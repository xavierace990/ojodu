WELCOME TO CHURCH OF CHRIST, OJODU

Our mission is to glorify God by bringing families and individuals of our community to a committed and growing relationship with Jesus Christ through ministry empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Our goal is to worship God in Spirit & in Truth. Our worship is organized in order to offer God an acceptable worship each time we meet. Our items of worship include: Singing, Praying, Teaching, Sermon, Communion, and Giving
